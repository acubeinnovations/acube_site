What we do exactly

Our service sector consists of the "soft" parts of the economy, i.e. activities where we offer people our knowledge and time to improve productivity, performance, potential, and sustainability, what is termed affective labor. Our basic characteristic in this sector is the production of services instead of end products. Services, also known as "intangible goods", include attention, advice, access, experience, and discussions.
